Inserting Audio Clips to a Slide

Step 1: Select the slide to which you want to add an audio effect.
Step 2: Click the 'Insert' tab.
Step 3: Click the Audio' button and then select 'Audio from File' from the drop-down list.
Step 4: An Insert Audio' window will appear on the screen.
Step 5: Browse the sound file that you want to add.
Step 6: Select your sound file and click 'Insert

Inserting your own Sound

Step 1: Select the slide on which you want to add your own sound.
Step 2: Click the 'Audio' button followed by the 'Record Audio' option list. The 'Record Sound' window will appear on the slide. It viz. play, stop and record.
Step 3: Click the 'Record' button. You will notice that the timer begins maintaining the sound length of your recording.
Step 4: Click 'OK' to stop recording. The 'Sound' icon will appear on your slide.

Inserting an Action Button

Step 1: Select the slide on which you want to insert an action button.
Step 2: Click the 'Insert' tab and then the 'Shapes' button in the 'Illustrations' group.
Step 3: Select an action button from the 'Action Buttons' section. The mouse pointer will take the shape of '+'sign.
Step 4: Bring the pointer on the selected slide.
Step 5: Drag the pointer to draw the shape of the button, ha 'hetion Setting didoy box will appear on the screen, it will display the selected button's action in the Hyperlink to box.
Step 6. Click on the 'Play sound box to play a sound with the action button.
Step 7: Click OK.
